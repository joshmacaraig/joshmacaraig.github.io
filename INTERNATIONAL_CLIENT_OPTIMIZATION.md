## 📅 **CRITICAL: Calendly Timezone Settings**

### **Step 1: Set Your Timezone Display**

1. Go to Calendly → **Account** → **Settings**
2. Find **"Timezone"** settings
3. **Critical choice:**

**Option A: Show YOUR timezone (Philippines)**
- Display: "Asia/Manila (GMT+8)"
- ✅ Honest and transparent
- ⚠️ Clients see times in your timezone (confusing)

**Option B: Let THEM see their timezone** ← **RECOMMENDED**
- Enable: **"Display times in invitee's timezone"**
- ✅ Each client sees times in their own timezone
- ✅ No confusion
- ✅ More bookings

**✅ Choose Option B!**
